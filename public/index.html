ECHOSAT — Tree Canopy Height

Per-point tree canopy height sampled from the ECHOSAT dataset (Pauls et al., AI4Forest / University of Münster) via Google Earth Engine.

  • Resolution: 10 m
  • Coverage: global, annual per-year layers 2018–2024
  • Accuracy: ~5 m mean absolute error (MAE)
  • Units: metres (converted from the source's centimetres)
  • License: CC-BY 4.0
  • Source: GEE asset projects/ai4forest/assets/echosat

Height is one of three MOLCA Forest/Shrubland criteria (also woody growth form and >50% canopy cover). Given the ~5 m MAE, treat values near the 5 m line as uncertain — use this as one clue, not a verdict.
